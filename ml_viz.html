Accountable Policing Project (NYC)

> View Code

This project seeks to improve police accountability and civilian access to information on delinquent law enforcement agents. I maintain a standardized database of police officers in New York City with records of past misconduct, providing an online interface that makes identifying police officers easier for the public. The web application is accompanied by statistical analyses and visualization to describe the relationship between violations of policy and procedure and disciplinary action. This project is in response to missing systems that would allow the public to effectively and safely identify problematic police officers.

Made with: Python/R/HTML/CSS/Javascript/jQuery
